About Us

When compared to the Land area under agriculture and the percentage of population engaged in this sector, the productivity of the agricultural sector is quite low. Apart from the yield, various factors like weather patterns, usage of fertilizers/pesticides and soil conditions also play an important role in production but these are often misinterpreted or remain unknown amongst the farmers owing to lack of information.

To provide timely, relevant & reliable information of major agricultural commodities to the farmers; for helping them in taking better decisions & maximizing farm income to achieve sustainability. To be a pioneer in the dissemination of information for benefit of the farmers & to be their friend at every stage of their cultivation.
